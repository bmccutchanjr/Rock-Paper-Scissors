Rock Paper Scissors

Instructions

This is a two-player implementation of the well known game Rock / Paper / Scissors. The game is pretty simple, but unfortuanely only allows two players and one game at a time.

The first thing you need to do is enter your name, whatever you want to be called.

The second thing you need to do is wait. The game can only be played when two players have connected to the server. You'll see a message prominanly displayed on your screen when both players have joined.

Then click on one of the Rock, Paper or Scissors icons. Rock breaks Scissors. Scissors cut Paper. And Paper covers Rock.

You can send a direct message to your opponent at any time.
